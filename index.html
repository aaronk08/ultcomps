FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our competitions

How do the competitions work?

Our competitions are simple: choose a prize you'd like to win, purchase tickets, and wait for the draw. The more tickets you buy, the better your chances of winning!

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected through a live draw that's streamed on our social media channels. We use a random number generator to ensure complete fairness and transparency.

How do I receive my prize if I win?

Winners are notified immediately after the draw. We arrange secure delivery of your prize, with full insurance and tracking. Most prizes are delivered within 7-14 days.

Are the competitions legal?

Yes, all our competitions are fully licensed and regulated. We operate under UK gaming laws and ensure complete compliance with all relevant regulations.

Can I get a refund on my tickets?

Tickets are non-refundable once purchased. However, if a competition is canceled for any reason, all ticket purchases will be fully refunded.

What happens if not all tickets are sold?

If a competition doesn't sell all its tickets, we have two options: extend the competition period or proceed with the draw and adjust the prize value proportionally.
